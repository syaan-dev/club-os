## 3. Personal data we collect, and why

| Category | What | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Identity & contact | Phone number, name, email | Account creation, OTP login, notifications, member identity |
| Profile (optional) | City/location, skills/interests, avatar photo | Enrich your club member profile and directory |
| Device contacts | Selected contacts' name + phone, **only when you choose to invite them** | Send club invitations. Not stored in bulk; used to create the specific invites you select |
| Device & push | Push token, device platform | Deliver push notifications |
| Financial | Dues, transactions, payment-link records | Club dues and payment collection (payments processed by Stripe) |
| Security | Audit events, consent records | Security, accountability, and proof of lawful consent |

We collect this data **directly from you** (and from your device, with your permission, for
contacts and push).

## 4. Legal basis — your consent

We process your personal data on the basis of your **consent**, which you give during
onboarding (and, for contacts and notifications, through device permission prompts). Your
consent is recorded with the policy version, time, and app context.

**You may withdraw consent at any time** — as easily as you gave it — by contacting us (§9) or
using the in-app controls. Withdrawing consent may mean you can no longer use parts of the
Service. Withdrawal does not affect processing already carried out lawfully.

## 5. How we share data (processors and partners)

We share personal data only with service providers ("**Data Processors**") who help us run the
Service, under appropriate safeguards:

- **Supabase** — database, authentication, file storage, and backend functions.
- **Stripe** — UPI and card payment processing (India / INR). We do not store full card numbers.
- **Expo / EAS, Apple (APNs), Google (FCM)** — delivery of push notifications.
- **Apple / Google** — app distribution.

We do **not** sell your personal data. Some providers may process data outside India; where
they do, we rely on the provider's safeguards as permitted under the DPDP Act.

## 6. Retention

We keep personal data for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the
Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. When no
longer required, we delete or anonymise it. Consent records are retained as needed to
demonstrate compliance.

## 7. Security

We use technical and organisational measures including encrypted transport, row-level access
controls in our database, and scoped storage permissions. No method of transmission or storage
is completely secure; we work to protect your data and to handle any personal-data breach in
accordance with the DPDP Act.

## 8. Your rights (DPDP Act)

- **Access** a summary of the personal data we process about you.
- **Correct, complete, or update** inaccurate or incomplete data.
- **Erase** your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
- **Grievance redressal** — raise a complaint with our Grievance Officer (§9).
- **Nominate** another individual to exercise your rights in the event of death or incapacity.

To exercise any right, contact us at **[CONTACT EMAIL]** or use the in-app **Setup → Legal**
