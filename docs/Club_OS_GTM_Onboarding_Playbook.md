# Club OS GTM Onboarding Playbook (India)

## 1. Objective

Onboard the first 50 clubs with a repeatable, trust-led acquisition process focused on dues collection outcomes.

Primary activation outcome per club:

- Club created
- Members invited
- First dues cycle created
- First payment received

---

## 2. Positioning and Core Message

### Core Promise

- Collect dues on time
- Keep transparent records
- Run meetings without chaos

### Tagline Options

- Club OS: Dues, Decisions, Trust.
- The operating system for Indian clubs.
- Stop chasing payments. Start running your club.

### ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

- Turf and sports clubs (10 to 300 members)
- Apartment sports and hobby groups
- College clubs and student chapters
- Community associations with recurring fees

---

## 3. 30-Day Marketing and Onboarding Calendar

## Week 1: Setup and Proof Assets (Days 1 to 7)

### Day 1

- Finalize landing page copy (problem-first)
- Create lead capture form (name, club type, city, member count, phone)

### Day 2

- Prepare demo environment with 1 sample club and realistic data

### Day 3

- Create 3 WhatsApp outreach templates (cold, warm, referral)

- Contact 20 clubs via WhatsApp and phone
- Book 5 live demos

### Day 6

- Run 2 to 3 demos
- Log objections and conversion blockers

### Day 7

- Publish first testimonial or pilot story
- Refine pitch deck and scripts based on objections

### Day 8

- Contact 20 new clubs

### Day 9

- Run 3 demos (focus on treasurer and secretary personas)
- Offer done-for-you setup in 24 hours

- Visit 2 local turf/academy partners for tie-ups
- Offer partner referral reward per activated club

### Day 12

- Run 2 demos + 1 onboarding workshop (small group)

- Push activation follow-up: create first dues cycle for newly onboarded clubs

### Day 14

## Week 3: Distribution and Partnerships (Days 15 to 21)

### Day 15

- Include social proof from first pilot users

### Day 19

- Publish case study #1 (before vs after dues collection)

### Day 20

- Run 3 demos and 3 onboarding calls

### Day 21

- Review funnel performance and tune messaging by segment

## Week 4: Scale What Works (Days 22 to 30)

### Day 22

- Start Meta click-to-WhatsApp campaign (only for best-performing segment)

### Day 23

- Start retargeting people who watched walkthrough video

### Day 24

- Run 3 demos and immediate onboarding for qualified clubs

### Day 25

- Publish case study #2 with quantitative outcomes

### Day 26

- Run partner check-in and incentivize top referrers

### Day 27

- Run activation drive: pending clubs complete first dues cycle

### Day 28

- Analyze CAC per activated club and lead-to-activation rates

### Day 29

- Create next 30-day city expansion plan

### Day 30

- Publish monthly performance report
- Decide: deepen current city or launch city 2

## 4. Channel Strategy and Budget Split (Starter)

Recommended for first month:

- 40% direct outreach (manual founder-led)
- 25% partnerships (turfs, academies, community admins)
- 20% micro-influencer and social proof assets
- 15% paid ads (only after message validation)

If budget is limited, prioritize direct outreach + partnerships over paid ads.

## 6. 10-Minute Demo Flow (High-Conversion)

## 0 to 1 min: Context

- Ask current process (WhatsApp/Excel/manual)
- Ask where leakage happens (missed payments, disputes, reminders)

## 1 to 3 min: Dues Setup

- Create club dues plan
- Show due date and member assignment

## 3 to 5 min: Member View

- Show member dashboard: due, paid, overdue
- Show clarity and transparency for all members

## 5 to 7 min: Reminder and Payment Flow

- Show reminder trigger and logs
- Show payment status updates and ledger sync

## 7 to 8 min: Governance Add-on

- Show quick meeting notes and vote flow (teaser)

## 8 to 9 min: Proof

- Share one pilot outcome (dues uplift/time saved)

## 9 to 10 min: Close

- Ask for go-live in 24 hours
- Collect onboarding info and set activation milestone

## 7. Objection Handling Sheet

## Objection 1: We already use WhatsApp and Excel

Response:
Totally fair. Club OS keeps your workflow simple but removes manual follow-up and confusion. You can still use WhatsApp, but with clear dues records and auto reminders behind it.

## Objection 2: Members will not install another app

Response:
We solve this by onboarding the core committee first. Once dues and reminders are active, members usually adopt because they get clear due status and proof of payment.

## Objection 3: We do not want complicated software

Response:
Setup is done-for-you in 24 hours. Your committee uses only 3 core actions initially: create dues, send reminders, track payments.

## Objection 4: Is payment data safe?

Response:
We use role-based access and club-level data isolation. Payment events are tracked with audit logs, so every record has traceability.

## Objection 5: Price is high

Response:
Core workflows can start on free/basic plan. Paid plan is for automation and advanced exports once value is proven.

## Objection 6: We need local language support

Response:
Yes, onboarding and reminder templates can be configured for local language communication.

## 8. Onboarding SOP (24-Hour Activation)

### Step 1: Discovery (15 min)

- Collect club profile, member count, dues frequency, committee contacts

### Step 2: Setup (30 to 60 min)

- Create club
- Import members
- Configure roles
- Create first dues cycle

### Step 3: Launch (15 min)

- Send invite links
- Trigger first reminder
- Guide treasurer on payment tracking screen

### Step 4: Day-7 Success Check

- Review pending vs paid
- Tune reminders
- Collect testimonial if successful

## 9. KPI Dashboard (Weekly)

Top-of-funnel:

- Leads generated
- Contacted leads
- Demo booked rate

Mid-funnel:

- Demo completion rate
- Demo to activation rate
- Time to first dues cycle

Activation and retention:

- Clubs with at least 1 completed dues cycle
- Dues collection uplift after 30 days
- Cost per activated club
- Referral rate

Suggested baseline targets for month 1:

- Demo booked rate: at least 20%
- Demo to activation: at least 35%
- Activation to first dues cycle: at least 70%
